 Company Profile

 Yamacho RMK Inc. is a food export and sales company founded in Hakodate City, Hokkaido. We are delivering the taste of Hokkaido to Asia and North America.

 About our Service

  • Bento Catering  We Provide Hokkaido Bento Boxes for Events and Parties. Our High Quality, Handmade Food Will Brighten up Your Special Day.
  • Event Exhibition  At Events We Offer Japanese Foods and Sweets (Warabi Mochi, Ohagi, Strawberry Candy). Event Attendants Enjoy our Traditional Japanese Food.
  • Importation and Wholesale of Seafood  We Import and Wholesale Fresh Hokkaido Seafood for Local Distribution. We Deliver High Quality Seafood.
  • Export Support  We Support Export Operations for Companies in Japan to Enter the Asian and North American Markets. Using Our Extensive Knowledge, We Help Our Customers Expand Their Business.

 About the Owner

  Isao Hasegawa Has Been Involved in International Trade for Many Years, Spreading the Taste of Hokkaido to the World.

Isao Hasegawa

Date of Birth: January, 1974
Birthplace: Hokkaido・Hakodate

Career Overview

Student Life

In high school, Isao Hasegawa became interested in American culture and traveled to America to study. Isao graduated from Seattle Shoreline Community College with a double major in Business and International Trade.

Start of Career

After returning to Japan, Isao joined WeNet Co., an advertising agency specializing in commercial alcohol marketing in Tokyo. He engaged in sales promotion for overseas brands and training of sales representatives.

Household Buisness

In 2002, Isao joined his family buisness, Yamacho Hasegawa Shoten Co., and held Hokkaido product exhibitions throughout Japan.

Expanding Oversees

In cooperation with a flour milling company in Hakodate, Isao held Hokkaido fairs at Isetan stores in Asian countries (Taiwan, Malaysia, Singapore, etc). He successfully held an Hokkaido exhibition at Hong Kong SOGO and established an overseas business (Yamacho Youkou Ltd. Hong Kong).

Expanding to the U.S.

Isao expanded Yamcho to America and established Yamacho U.S.A. Inc. In 2020, Isao became independent and launched "Yamacho RMK Inc.", and currently sells Japanese ingredients and delicatessen dishes at Mitsuwa Market in California.
